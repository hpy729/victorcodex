material story

A brighter single serve experience with a lower impact story.

Tonchant Coffee Filters can be specified with biodegradable paper or non-woven filter material options, helping coffee brands build convenient products without giving up their sustainability message.

why the disc format

More stable than classic hanging ear bags, more distinctive on shelf.

The recognisable round support ring rests across the cup rim while the center filter pocket holds coffee grounds. The result is a clean single serve ritual with more surface area for branding than a standard drip bag.

The ring supports the filter.

The wide disc sits across the cup opening, helping the filter pocket stay open and reducing the uneven pull often seen in classic hanging ear bags.

The center pocket handles extraction.

Ground coffee sits inside the center filter pocket. Hot water passes through the coffee bed and filters directly into the cup or server.

The format improves brand presentation.

The top ring, lid, outer pouch, and display box can all support a premium visual system for retail, hotel, travel, and tasting programs.

consumer ritual

Easy for the consumer. Flexible for your product team.

The filter, coffee dose, and outer pouch can be prepared in advance. Consumers only need a cup and hot water, making the format suitable for cafes, offices, hotels, travel kits, and promotional tasting boxes.

01

Open the pouch

Remove the individually packed filter and keep the disc ring and center pocket open.

02

Place on cup

Set the round support ring flat on the cup rim so the filter pocket hangs inside.

03

Pour in stages

Bloom the grounds, then add hot water according to your brand's brew recipe.

04

Lift and discard

After filtration is complete, lift out the filter for a cleaner single serve routine.
